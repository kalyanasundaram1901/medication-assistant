--- PARACETAMOL ---
Name: Paracetamol
Used for: Fever and mild to moderate pain
How it works: Reduces pain signals and body temperature
Common side effects: Nausea, liver damage if overdosed
Notes: Do not exceed prescribed dose

--- CETIRIZINE ---
Name: Cetirizine
Used for: Allergy symptoms like sneezing and itching
How it works: Blocks histamine
Common side effects: Drowsiness, dry mouth
Notes: Avoid alcohol

--- AZITHROMYCIN ---
Name: Azithromycin
Used for: Bacterial infections
How it works: Stops bacterial growth
Common side effects: Stomach upset, diarrhea
Notes: Complete full course
